About Dr. Abu Sufian

Professional Biography

I am Dr. Abu Sufian, a researcher (Senior Fellowship) at the National Research Council of Italy (CNR), Institute of Applied Sciences and Intelligent Systems (CNR-ISASI). Since 2024, I have been contributing to the national consortium project Future Artificial Intelligence Research (FAIR, 2023–2025), focusing on the development of responsible and trustworthy AI systems.

I have over 14 years of academic experience, including nine years as an Assistant Professor at the University of Gour Banga, India, where I also served as Head of the Department of Computer Science and led curriculum development initiatives. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Visva-Bharati University, India.

My research is driven by the goal of making AI more equitable and transparent. I work at the intersection of Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Generative AI, with applications in Biometrics, Media Forensics, and Healthcare, emphasizing AI Fairness, Bias Mitigation, and Explainability. I believe impactful innovation emerges from interdisciplinary collaboration, and I actively engage with researchers across diverse domains.

I have authored 50+ peer-reviewed publications in leading venues, including IEEE Access, Pattern Recognition, Knowledge-Based Systems, ICCV, and ECCV, with 2,600+ citations (Google Scholar). I am a Senior Member of IEEE and a Lifetime Member of the Computer Society of India, and I actively contribute to the research community through international workshops and professional service.
