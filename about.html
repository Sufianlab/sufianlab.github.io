About Dr. Abu Sufian

Professional Biography

I am Dr. Abu Sufian, a researcher (Senior Fellowship) at the National Research Council of Italy (CNR), Institute of Applied Sciences and Intelligent Systems (CNR-ISASI). Since 2024, I have been contributing to the national consortium project Future Artificial Intelligence Research (FAIR, 2023–2025), focusing on responsible and trustworthy AI systems.

I have over 14 years of academic experience, including nine years as an Assistant Professor at the University of Gour Banga, India, where I also served as Head of the Department of Computer Science and led curriculum development initiatives. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Visva-Bharati University, India.

My research lies at the intersection of Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Generative AI, with applications in Biometrics, Media Forensics, and Healthcare. My work emphasizes AI Fairness, Bias Mitigation, and Explainability, aiming to develop equitable and transparent AI systems.

I have authored 50+ peer-reviewed publications in venues such as IEEE Access, Pattern Recognition, Knowledge-Based Systems, ICCV, and ECCV, accumulating 2,600+ citations (Google Scholar). I am a Senior Member of IEEE and a Lifetime Member of the Computer Society of India.
