Research & Publication Profiles

⦿ I am an academic driven by a motivation to make artificial intelligence more responsible, transparent, and equitable. My research focuses on Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Generative AI, with applications in Media Forensics, Biometrics, and Healthcare, examined through the lens of AI Fairness, Bias Mitigation, and Explainability.

⦿ I believe that the most impactful innovations emerge at the intersection of disciplines and meaningful discussions. Accordingly, I am deeply committed to engaging with domain experts and researchers across diverse fields to push the boundaries of what is technically possible, while ensuring that AI systems are designed to serve all sections of society fairly and responsibly.
